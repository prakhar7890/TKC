About TKC

The Kattori Chammach is built for variety, comfort, and late-night dining.

TKC serves a broad pure veg menu for families, students, friend groups and quick takeaway orders. The space is positioned as a casual restaurant with a cafe-like mix of comfort food, full meals and snacks.

Restaurant Identity

One stop for mixed cravings.

The menu combines chaap, Chinese, burgers, dosa, pasta, curries, soups, breads, biryani and desserts. That variety is the core TKC identity: one place where different diners can order different things without compromise.

The restaurant is designed around repeat local visits, evening meetups, late-night appetite and familiar comfort dishes.
